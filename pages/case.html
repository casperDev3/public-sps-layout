What we did?

A customer reached out to us seeking assistance in building an administrative dashboard tailored for their food delivery business. They expressed dissatisfaction with existing market applications that failed to meet their company's requirements adequately.

Over time, this initial request evolved into the conception of a comprehensive CRM system designed specifically for food delivery enterprises and their suppliers.
